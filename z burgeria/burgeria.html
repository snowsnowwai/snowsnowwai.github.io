AUDREY'S BURGERIA

P5.JS, HTML
800 px by 600 px

Play Audrey's Burgeria by using your numberpad, as each number has a corresponding ingredient. Click or hold down your mouse when you want to create your burger.

Growing up, I was obsessed with Cool Math Games - I loved playing games like Fireboy and Watergirl, Run, and the Papa's Franchise. For this project I wanted to do something nostalgic and put my own spin on it. When brainstroming about what I wanted to make, a game from the Papa's Franchise seemed perfect. I chose Papa's Burgeria because I liked the variation of textures and colors the Burgeria has.
