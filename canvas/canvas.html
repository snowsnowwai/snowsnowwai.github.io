Draw with this Etch a Sketch by clicking the color, brush size, and let your creativity flow! If one of your drawings catches your eye, right click the canvas to save the image. If you want to start over, click the clear button to wipe the slate clean.

Growing up, I was enthralled by Etch a Sketch. Playing with it in stores and seeing it in movies like Toy Story left me wanting more. It seemed like magic, watching my drawings appear and disappear. I wanted to emulate that and elevate a regular drawing canvas tool.
