May your life be as bright as the full moon and your heart as sweet as mooncakes!

Mooncakes have a significant impact on Chinese culture. They symbolize unity of families and good fortune. This project centers around that and has three parts. The first part was to create the mooncake design in Minecraft. For that, I used stripped oak logs and brown wool. I then exported that design and put it into FlashPrint. From there, I scaled the model and printed. The first model turned out too small and simple for my liking. I went back into Minecraft and repeated the process. Creating a more complex and larger design was difficult because I didn't want to make too complex of a design. I also scaled the second version up more so the details are more noticeable.
