GLITCHED

Notepad ++
Various sizes

For these images, I used Notepad ++ to glitch and modify photos of Frida Kahlo, Marie Curie, and Rosa Parks. I chose impactful and well-known women as my subjects to highlight them. While all of the women's portraits are distorted, their faces and features are still somewhat recognizable and distinct, glitching each woman differently.

Kahlo's photo is staggered and fractured, almsot as if the image is showing on a faulty VHS tape. Curie's photo is extremely distorted, almost as if the image was corrupted by her radiation. Parks' photo is heavily disrupted and blotchy, almost as if the image was damaged by water.
