A DAY IN MY GLAMOROUS LIFE

My name is Jade. Meow you doing?

To interact with this project, click on the images to go to the next page. Clicking on the last image will take you back to the home page.

This is my older cat, Jade. I often think about how different yet similar our day to days are and how my life would be like if I were a cat. To try to answer these curiosities, I made this to mimic a day in Jade's life, putting you in her paws.
