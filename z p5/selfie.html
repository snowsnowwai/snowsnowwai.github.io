SOUR AUDREY 1.0

P5.JS, HTML
800 px by 800 px

This character draws heavily from Sour Bill from Wreck-It Ralph. I like the contrast between his whimsical character design and his "sour" personality. I added my own features and characteristics to make it my own. I especially tried to mimic Sour Bill's spherical nature and gloomy emotion.
