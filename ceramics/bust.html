SPIRALING

Clay
Approx 8 in by 6 in by 6.5 in

In this ceramic sculpture, I tried to emulate my features and emotions, inspired by Tim Burton and Laika Studios. I enjoy their macabre but somewhat simple appearances paired with expressive features. My sculpture encapsulates how I view myself. The missing mouth and furrowed eyebrows symbolize my feelings of being judged and worrying about how others perceive me. The skull is open, emphasizing vulnerability and introspection. The spiral pattern represents my tendency to overthink and get lost in my thoughts, as well as the cyclical nature of my emotions.
