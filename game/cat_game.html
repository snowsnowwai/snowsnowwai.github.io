HUNGRY HUNGRY CAT

P5.JS, HTML
800 px by 800 px

Play Hungry Hungry Cat by clicking the cat treats and feeding my hungry cat, Jade, until she's full. As the game progresses, the treat becomes smaller and smaller.

As a child, I loved Hungry Hungry Hippos. It was one of my favorite games to play with my family because it fed my competitive side. I wanted to create a game that was inspired by it, but with a personal twist. My cats are a huge part of my life and I wanted to incorporate them into my project.
